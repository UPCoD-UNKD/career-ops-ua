## Tier 1 — Industry Awards & Institutional Citations

### RTS Innovative News Award — The Stream (2012)
- **Source:** Al Jazeera English (aljazeera.com)
- **URL:** https://www.aljazeera.com/features/2012/2/23/al-jazeera-english-named-channel-of-the-year
- **Date:** February 23, 2012
- **Award:** Royal Television Society (RTS) — Innovative News Award
- **What it validates:** The Stream's social media integration workflow (Twitter, Facebook, YouTube, Skype) recognized by the RTS as industry-leading innovation in news broadcasting. Al Jazeera English also won RTS News Channel of the Year the same year.
- **Key quote (Paul Eedle, Director of Programmes):** "The Stream allowed people to share their experiences, air their views and give opinions."
- **Additional context:** Al Jazeera English also won the George Polk Award (journalism's equivalent of the Pulitzer) for "Shouting in the Dark" the same year, and the Alfred I. duPont-Columbia University Award. The RTS award specifically names Bahrain protests coverage as the basis — the same multi-platform social integration Mitchell was part of as founding AP.
- **Use in:** Industry Impact document (Claim 1 — The Stream section), cover letters for editorial/AI-native roles, LinkedIn About section.

## How to Use

When writing cover letters, pull the RTS citation specifically:

That sentence: one credential, one year, one specific award, one specific claim. That's the right density for a cover letter.

For the Industry Impact document: use Tier 1 citations inline, Tier 2 as footnotes/links, Tier 3 as supporting context.
